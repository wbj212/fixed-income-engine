Risk Commentary

The distribution displays Right-Skewness, typical of mean-reverting interest rate models where lower bounds are constrained. CVaR provides the average magnitude of tail events beyond the 99% threshold.

Methodology & Engine Mechanics

Mathematical Foundation

The engine utilizes a Vasicek Model to simulate interest rate movements. This model is crucial for fixed income as it captures the mean-reverting nature of interest rates, preventing simulated yields from drifting infinitely.

dr(t) = a(b - r(t))dt + σdW(t)

Implementation Details

  • 01/ 10,000 Path Monte Carlo processed via NumPy for tail-risk accuracy.
  • 02/ VaR vs. CVaR: Calculated as the 99th percentile cutoff and the average loss of the worst 100 scenarios, respectively.
